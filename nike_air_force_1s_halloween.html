        "Halloween"

  • Halloween is just over a week away, and the industry’s top sneaker brands have been getting you ready with a deluge of spooky kicks for All Hallow’s Eve.
    From the affectionately dubbed Off-White Nike Blazer “Spooky” Pack, to a slime-ridden pair of Kyrie 4s, the Swoosh has been paving the way with high profile drops for every young sneaker fanatic’s favorite fall celebration. Perhaps the most season of mischief-appropriate shoe of the bunch is a special Air Force 1 Low equipped with skeleton-style embellishments meant to mimic the wearer’s foot adorning its upper.
    Acting as an x-ray of sorts, this AF1 Low’s insoles sport a matching grey, air-brushed graphic while its Nike tongue tag swaps its regular font out for even more bones.
    Currently, this pair is scheduled to arrive at retailers on Halloween itself, meaning you are going to have to look elsewhere for the perfect piece of footwear to complete your Grim Reaper get-up.
