        "Platinum Tint"

  • After extensive previews as well as our own YouTube unboxing of the shoes, the Air Jordan 11 “Platinum Tint” is finally releases tomorrow, October 27th, 2018.
    Staying in line with the Jumpman’s penchant for dropping the Jordan 11 around holiday season, this creamy rendition of one of the most revered silhouette’s in the Jumpman’s history offers up a touch of history with its release.
    While fans of the 1996 original tend to adore the model’s customary slide-out box and premium accessories, the Platinum Tints opt to revert back to the original sneaker’s box and black tissue lining.
    Another major deviation from the norm comes by way of its mudguard, as the customary patent leather material gets swapped out with a less flashy nubuck.
    While hype surrounding this drop isn’t even in the same ballpark as December’s Concord 11 re-release, stock is still expected to go quick, being that it is a Jordan 11 after all.
