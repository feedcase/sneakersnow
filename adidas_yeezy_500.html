  • ADIDAS YEEZY
          500




        "Utility Black"   "Salt"

  • Adidas and Kanye West’s long-term partnership is continuing, as a brand-new “Salt” colorway of the chunky adidas Yeezy 500 is set to release for their customary retail price of $200 USD.
    Continuing the single-color/minimal branding look that ‘Ye has utilized for his previous Yeezy 500 releases like the “Utility Black” and “Super Moon Yellow,” the “Salt” 500s call upon a light grey color for the entire sneaker — from the paneled mesh and leather upper to the chunky midsole inspired by retro adidas basketball shoes.
    As the only model in the adidas Yeezy line that doesn’t use a bouncy Boost cushioning system, the Salt Yeezys instead turn to a classic adiPRENE technology for exemplary comfort.
    With many cold winter days ahead of us, the “Salt” colorway is perfect for the season, so be sure to check out our adidas Yeezy store list to get ready for the release.
